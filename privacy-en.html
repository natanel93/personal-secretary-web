Privacy Policy

Version 2.0 · Effective 21 July 2026
← גרסה עברית
The short version, without lawyers: Noga reads what you connect to her in order to help you, and uses an external AI model to understand it. Your email bodies are never stored with us. We do not sell your data, we do not share it with advertisers, and we never send anything on your behalf without your approval. You can delete your account and data at any time, and the deletion is real.

1. Who is responsible for your data

Service operator and data controller: Natanel Malachi, Israel. Privacy contact: natanel.malachi@gmail.com.

Noga is a digital "personal manager": she reads sources you connect, proposes actions, and manages tasks and reminders for you.

International transfers

Your data is stored in the European Union. Some sub-processors process data in the United States; those transfers rely on the EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and the providers' own commitments. We are working to move AI processing to an EU-based endpoint in order to reduce such transfers.

6. How long we keep your data

Full disclosure: as of this version, data you create is kept until you delete it or delete your account — there is not yet an automatic time-based deletion. We are currently building automatic deletion of message content, and when it ships we will update this document with exact periods. Email bodies, as stated, are never stored in the first place.
  • Billing records are retained after account deletion, stripped of personal identifiers, as required by tax and accounting law.
  • The security log is kept for a limited period; on account deletion the identifiers in it (email address, IP) are erased.
  • Backups may contain a copy for a short period after deletion, until the next backup cycle.

7. Your rights

At any time you may access your data, correct it, erase it, export it in a machine-readable format, restrict or object to certain processing, and withdraw consent you have given.

  • Self-service export and deletion: in the app, under "My account" — a full JSON export, and immediate, complete deletion of your account and all data.
  • Deleting a single person: you can remove any person Noga saved, directly from the screen or by asking Noga.
  • For any other request, contact us and we will respond within 30 days.

Right to complain: if you are not satisfied, you may lodge a complaint with the Israeli Privacy Protection Authority, or with the supervisory authority in your EU country of residence.

8. Security

  • Encryption in transit and at rest; every account is isolated at the database level.
  • Mailbox passwords are encrypted (AES-256) with a key that is not stored in the database.
  • Inbound channels are cryptographically signature-verified; an unconfigured channel is closed, not open.
  • A security event log that clients cannot modify.

No system is perfectly secure. In the event of a security incident that puts your rights at risk, we will notify you and the authorities as required by law.

9. Age

The service is intended for people aged 16 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children below that age.

10. Changes

We will update this document as the product changes. A material change will be shown in the app before it takes effect. This version supersedes all previous ones.
